<h2>Short Analysis Report</h2>
<p>
An analysis of the population data indicates that the growth pattern is exponential rather than linear, as evidenced by the growth rate increasing annually from 950 to 1,450 individuals per year rather than remaining constant. Within this period, growth accelerated most significantly in 2023, reaching a rate of 1,450 individuals per year. Based on these trends and applying a linear regression model, the projected population for 2025 is estimated to be 15,740 individuals.
